Prince Louis Rwagasore

Former Prime Minister of Burundi

Biography

Prince Louis Rwagasore was a Burundian politician who served as the second prime minister of Burundi for a brief period from September 28, 1961, until his assassination. He was a prominent figure in the country's independence movement and was murdered just over 60 years ago, which involved the complicity of Belgium during a tumultuous period in Burundi's history.
